About Me

Taber Haislip is an Atlanta native with background in Music, Technical Theatre, Art, and numerous other skills and passions. A graduate of Parkview High School in Gwinnett County, he also attended Dekalb School of The Arts (DSA), where he majored in Theatrical Lighting Design. Prior to attending DSA, Taber interned at 7 Stages Theatre, a local non-profit performance space in the Little 5 Points district, located on Atlanta's east side. Taber is also known to tinker with 70s era pedal start mopeds, and currently rides with the Swoop Jockeys MPC(moped club). He has recently taken the initiative to dive into the world of coding through the Georgia Tech Coding Bootcamp, using said skills to create this portfolio page.
